If you have received an in-app notification for a tax legislation update, please tap on the “Get tax legislation update” button.

An update to legislation will take approximately 1 to 2 minutes to download.

Please do not close the app until the update is complete.

We recommend performing updates using a stable Wi-Fi connection.

If you have received an in-app notification for a tax information update, please tap on the “Get tax information update” button.

An update to tax information will take approximately 3 to 10 minutes to download.

Please do not close the app until the update is complete.

We recommend performing updates using a stable Wi-Fi connection.
